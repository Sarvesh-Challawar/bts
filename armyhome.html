About ARMY

BTS fans are now officially called A.R.M.Y (아미), which stands for Adorable Representative M.C for Youth. They also explained the significant meaning behind it. ARMY is military in English, the body armor and military are always together, so it means that the fans will always be together with the Bangtan Boys. Jul 9, 2013, this was the announcement from BTS , but I believe BIGHIT Ent. - Bang Si-hyuk is who came up with the name not sure.

The official ARMY name is used after the BTS has it in the fancafe on 9 July 2013. Because of the BTS fandom named ARMY, BigHit uses this day each year by giving surprise events to ARMY who have not leveled up in the fancafe.

However, as time went on the meaning took a turn and now means something more for BTS. For BTS the ARMY means, they help, protect and support BTS. ARMY literally means army, people who stand against and up for what is right for them, and that is what we ARMY do and we stand up with BTS and defend, help and guard them (more in a metaphorical term).

The meaning I love the most (author unknown) is:
" a person that will always love and protect BTS from all bad things in the world."
