Stage Name: Suga a.k.a Agust D
Real Name: Min Yoon Gi
Position: Lead rapper
Date of Brith: March 9, 1993
Brithplace: Buk-gu, Daegu, South Korea
Height: 174 cm (5'8.5")
Weight: 59 Kg (130 lbs)
Zodiac sign: Pisces
Blood group: O
Education: Global Cyber University
Degree: Liberal Arts Major
Favorite number: 3
Favorite color: White
Favourite food: Meat
Family: Parents and older brother
Hobbies: Taking photos, doing-nothing and skipping work

Facts:

  1. He uses the nickname ‘Agust D’ when making and producing music. The nickname comes from spelling his stage name in reverse (SUGA – AGUS) then adding the initials of his former rap group before joining BTS (D-Town or Town Daegu).

  2. As a music producer, he has around 70 songs credited to him.

  3. He composes songs and lyrics in his studio named ‘Genius Lab’.

  4. Suga produces one song per day, which impresses RM, as he only makes one song a month.To which suga refuses and says he produces 200 songs a year.

  5. In 2017, he won an award for producing the song “Wine” for Korean singer Suran and has earned a spot as a full-time member of the Korea Music Copyright Association(KOMCA).

  6. As a rapper, he won 2nd place at a national rap audition in Korea. This event got the attention of Big Hit and signed with the label when he was 18 years old.

  7. When he signed with Big Hit, he was originally told that he would be part of a rap group. Upon his move from the underground rap scene, he received harsh criticisms from his former group mates and many underground rappers.

  8. He is Known to be intimidating and savage, he is very blunt towards his fellow members. However, despite looking cold, he shows care in the guise of small actions.

  9. Suga has a knack for repairing broken stuff, which is usually caused by RM.

Real Name: Jung Ho Seok
Position: Main dancer, Rapper, Sub-vocalist
Date of Brith: February 18, 1994
Brithplace: Gwangju, Jeolanamdo, South Korea
Height: 177 cm (5'10")
Weight: 65 Kg (143 lbs)
Zodiac sign: Aquarius
Blood group: A
Education: Gwangju Global High School and Global Cyber University
Favorite number: 7
Favorite color: Green
Favorite season: Spring
Favourite food: Kimchi
Family: Parents and older sister
Hobbies: Window shopping and listening to music

Facts:

  1. His stage name came from his desire to become a symbol of hope and a bringer of peace to everyone.

  2. Before joining BTS, J-Hope was known as the best freestyle street dancer in his hometown. Fellow member V confirmed this when a fellow student at Korea Arts School asked him after his debut with BTS if J-Hope was the same guy from Gwangju.

  3. As a dancer, he learns the dance steps faster than anyone on the group and usually acts as the dance leader.

  4. However, despite his sunny personality, he is very strict when teaching dance moves. Apart from learning and teaching dance steps, he also adds his steps to the choreography, showing his skill as a dancer.

  5. As a rapper, he once collaborated on the song “Animal” with 2 AM’s Jokwon.

  6. He auditioned for Big Hit while he was 17 years old, and was a trainee for 3 years under the label. During his training days, he found the process too difficult and left the company at least once.

  7. RM reached out to him to resolve the issue, bringing him back to the group. To know more about what their fans are thinking, J-Hope visits their fan café (an online messaging board) every once in a while.

  8. He has a thing for cleaning other member’s rooms, as well as keeping his room the cleanest. Because of this, along with his habit of scolding fellow members, he is called “Eomma” or mother.

Stage Name: RM
Real Name: Kim Nam Joon
Position: Leader, Main rapper
Date of Brith: September 12, 1994
Brithplace: Ilsan, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea
Height: 181.68 cm (5’11”)
Weight: 67 kg (147 lbs)
Zodiac sign: Virgo
Blood group: A
Education: Apgujeong High School and Global Cyber University
Favorite number: 1
Favorite color: Purple, Black and Pink
Favourite food: Meat (Samgyeopsal), and Korean noodles (Kalguksu)
Family: Parents and younger sister
Hobbies: Web surfing

Facts:

  1. He was the first member and current leader of the group. As a leader, he handles conflict resolution between the group and management, as well as disputes within the group. He also keeps the team focused and united during performances.

  2. Among the group, he is the only member that is fluent in English. He takes on interpretation and translation for the group during interviews, as well as coaches them on how to speak the language.

  3. According to RM, he learned how to speak English by watching the American show “Friends”.

  4. Apart from English, he is also fluent in Japanese and can easily learn foreign languages.

  5. In connection with his vocabulary, the rapper is known for his extensive vocabulary and wordplay that he employs with his songs. He also can rap using complex beats.

  6. RM also received criticism from underground rappers for choosing to stay with BTS, often told that he betrayed hip-hop, and was the content of some diss tracks from rappers. In his mixtape titled “RM”, he talks about the criticisms, along with the identity crisis he has as a rapper and a pop idol.

  7. Joining Big Hit in 2009, he was originally told the group would be a hip hop act, which was his reason for joining the label. He trained for 3 years under the label, until the debut of the group.

  8. As a producer, he has at least 100 songs under his name. This includes a track on the Korean release of Fantastic Four, among others.

  9. As a rapper and performer, he has collaborated with several international acts, including Krizz Kaliko, Warren G, Wale and Fall Out Boy.

  10. RM spoke on behalf of the group during their historic speech at the 73rd United Nations General Assembly. Marked as the launch of UNICEF’s ‘Generation Unlimited’, the rapper encouraged everyone to find their voice by speaking up regardless of race, gender or color.

  11. He earned the nickname “God of Destruction” as he seems to destroy anything he touches.

  9. The Hulk is his favorite character from the Avengers because he can destroy everything with just his bare hands.

  10. His motto is “Let’s keep on trying until we can’t do it anymore”.

Favorite season: Fall

Facts:

  1. The stage name V has a meaning, and it stands for ‘Victory’.

  2. He joined Big Hit at 17 years old and was training under the label for 2 years before their debut. His audition was a complete accident. Originally, his friends were the ones auditioning for the entertainment label, but he was seen by the staff and was asked to audition as well.

  3. Before the official debut of the group, BTS was thought to be a 6-man group with V being a secret member. However, he was announced as the 7th member to the surprise of the group’s fans.

  4. Apart from his work with the group, V has appeared in the historical drama “Hwarang” as an actor. His role on the show earned ridicule among his fellow members, with his acting skills being the target of their jokes.

  5. He has a puppy named ‘Tan-e’, derived from the first name of the group, Bangtan.

  6. The singer’s interests include fine art and photography. One of his weird habits is giving a false name when buying stuff on foreign locations of Starbucks and supermarkets. His usual names include Renoir, Van Gough, and other artists.

  7. An avid fan of amusement parks, he likes exciting rides like Gyro Swing, Drop Zone, and Roller Coasters.

  8. According to Korean culture, his is blood type (AB) has the attributes of being a genius or a psychopath.
